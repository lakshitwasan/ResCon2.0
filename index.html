UNDERGRADUATE PROJECT EXPO

The Undergraduate Project Expo (UPE) is an opportunity for students to present their published or unpublished research paper. There are four panels catering to varied interests and will evaluate two topics each and select the top 10 papers from each panel to present to our esteemed faculty panel.

ROUND TABLE CONFERENCE

Participants will receive a problem statement and devise a solution for it. They must show their comprehension of the problem during registration by submitting a brief summary. A committee of Ph.D. scholars will select the top 10 students to attend Bennett University.

WORKSHOPS

6 experts and institutes from varied fields with expansive knowledge will be invited to hold interactive workshops on upcoming developments in their respective fields. The workshops will provide a platform for students to learn and expand their knowledge.

RESEARCH STARTUP MARATHON

Students can form teams of up to 3 members and register their teams for Rs. 300. Participants can choose a problem for the team and develop a research-based startup idea. After completion of 24 hours, the startup will then be pitched to a judge panel. Participate for exciting cash prizes and funding for your projects.

READX

ReadX is a grass roots initiative that invites ingenious minds to showcase the myriad of experiences. This event will feature 4 speakers sharing their unique experiences, which can be described as 4 worlds colliding to create a new big bang of ideas and insights.

ABOUT BURS

Bennett Undergraduate Research Society (BURS), is a student-led organisation functioning with unparalleled support from the Dean of Research and Consultancy, Dr. Rama Komaragiri, and the Vice-Chancellor, Prof Prabhu Kumar Aggarwal. BURS encourages the undergraduate students to push their limits and become researchers at a young age.

ABOUT RESCON

Bennett Undergraduate Research Society (BURS)  aspires to assist undergraduate researchers and cultivate both the fundamental knowledge and attitude for research. With this, we are delighted to announce the second research conclave, BU RESCON 2.0 at Bennett University (The Times Group), which will take place on the 15th , 16th and 17th  of April 2023. We intend on conducting multiple research oriented events to encourage students to participate and present their research work and win multiple monetary prizes and goodies.
